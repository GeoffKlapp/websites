About Me

I am a current student in the CS department (surprise, surprise) of UMass Lowell with aspirations to develop the skills required to not just consume content on the Internet, but to create content for others to use, distribute, and enjoy. Prior experience with coding before college was minimal (so minimal I only ever looked into HTML briefly) so every language has been a new experience. So far, the most fun I've had with any language awkwardly enough has been with Scheme in OPL. Something just clicked during the assignments and using Dr.Racket to design from scratch a working version of the card game War. I have also enjoyed taking Professor Chen's Android Development course, but the task of teaching myself enough Java to succeed was more pressure than I had imagined. Thanks to that experience, however, now I try to study further into Android and Java in my spare time at home, with the goal to release an application with positive reviews for both personal satisfaction and as a demonstration of abilities for any job offerings.

When I am not in the classroom, I am either working as a Computer Sales representative at Best Buy (at least the topic is tech) or I am fulfilling my duties as an Internet warrior on my rig at home. Gaming, listening to music, watching videos, reading, or anything else imaginable in pixel format are normally my daily activities. I am always logged into Steam and normally play Counter Strike or my personal favorite: TERA.
